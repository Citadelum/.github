## About Citadelum

Citadelum is a city-building strategy game inspired by ancient Roman civilization. Players design and develop settlements, manage resources, and expand their influence across growing territories.

The game focuses on building infrastructure, maintaining citizen satisfaction, and balancing economic growth with expansion. Strategic planning is essential as players construct buildings, manage supply chains, and ensure the stability of their cities.
